Getting Started

1
Position yourself in front of the camera with good lighting
2
Ensure your hand is clearly visible in the camera frame
3
Start making ASL signs - letters will appear automatically

Making Signs

1
Form clear ASL letter signs (A-Z)
2
Hold each sign steady for 2 seconds
3
The letter will be added to your sentence once recognized
Tip: Keep your hand movements smooth and steady for best results

Adding Spaces

1
To add a space between words, remove your hand from the camera
2
Keep your hand away for 4 seconds
3
A space will automatically be added OR use the Space button
Manual Control: You can also use the "Add Space" button anytime!

Smart Features

1
Suggestions: Click on suggested words to auto-complete
2
Text-to-Speech: Click "Speak" to hear your sentence
3
Quick Actions: Backspace, Space, and Clear buttons
History: All transcriptions are saved automatically - click to copy!

Voice Recognition (Web Speech API)

1
Click "Start Recording" to begin voice capture
2
Speak clearly into your microphone
3
Click "Stop Recording" to save the transcription
Note: Works best in Chrome and Edge browsers
